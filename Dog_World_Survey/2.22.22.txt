I have been a fool, and a vain one! I felt lonely and with intention sought to cure my loneliness with another person. In my attempt, all I was met with was the soulless, anxious eyes of the real woman I had projected my ideal woman onto. I wished for a person who I could see myself in, like some perverted god, and wished to be happy by seeing my reflection. How I have been saved by a dull mirror!  When I looked into these eyes I understood the world did not hold what I really needed. A true woman does not come from  the world, not as a reflection of man, but from God as a reflection of God. Therefore I must be built up by my loneliness, with fortitude and resolve, and only take what I am given without seeking by my will. I do not feel sorry for my intention; I do feel it to be a necessary step in my life to become involved in what I do. Still, I must be the obsessed shepherd of my intention, and leave life up to God where He must take control. This is my new decision. From now on I will be perfectly intentional in everything I do. Nothing will be left to impulse. I don't type like this to give off some false air of swagger, and I don't mean to be pretentious.
